Frequently asked questions

How do I create and send an email campaign?

You can create an email campaign by choosing a template, adding your content, and scheduling it to send. It only takes a few minutes.

Do you have tools for list segmentation and personalization?

Yes! You can segment contacts by location, engagement, or interests, and personalize emails using tags like first name or company.

How much does Constant Contact cost?

Pricing depends on your plan and contact list size. Free trials are available to help you get started.
